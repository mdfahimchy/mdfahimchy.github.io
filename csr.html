CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY


MGH Foundation was set up to assist Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs), individuals and organizations to eradicate child labor and provide guidance, direction and help to improve lives of under-privileged children.

MGH Foundation also provides Legal Aid to the unprivileged prisoners who are in false custody and are financially incapable of availing proper legal support. Legal Aid Society for the Unfortunates (LASUF) has been formed to give an opportunity to those falsely accused.

The CSR initiatives are aimed at eradicating child labor, empowering women, providing legal aid, rehabilitation, social awareness programs etc.

MGH Scholarship Fund

In January 2008, an MOU was signed between MGH and The Asian University for Women to provide scholarships to deserving student.

Recent Cases

  • In September 2009, Shushanto Sutrodhor, son of a security guard almost lost his opportunity in continuing study due to financial constraint. MGH came forward to provide him with the means to continue his endeavor towards a better future.
  • MGH is also collaborating with Bangladesh Society for Child Neurology and Development & Disability (BSCNDD) to provide an equal opportunity for all children.
  • A poor girl named Nipa from Jessore had a road accident and sustained spinal injury leading to complete paralysis of the lower body. MGH is now helping the girl back to her feet through medical rehabilitation.
