Nando's

Nando’s was born in 1987 in South Africa, although the tale of Nando’s and our inspirational Peri-Peri takes us back much further in time.

From the humble beginnings, Nando’s Restaurants have now opened in 34 countries on five continents around the world, launched in Dhaka in August, 2007.

MGH Restaurants Pte. Limited is the Master Franchisee of Nandos in Bangladesh, and in Nepal. With the first 550 square meter sprawling Flag Ship Store opened in Dhanmondi, Dhaka, MGH opened the second 550 square meter store in Gulshan in 2009, the Premium Suburb of Bangladesh.
